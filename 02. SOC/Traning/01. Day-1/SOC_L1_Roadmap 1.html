Why SOC Exists

A Security Operations Center (SOC) is the heart of enterprise cyber defense. It helps organizations detect, investigate, and respond to threats — 24x7. Without a SOC, breaches go undetected, costing millions in damage, data loss, and reputation.

  • Protects sensitive data and systems in real time
  • Reduces breach costs by early detection & response
  • Complements NOC (network uptime) and IR (post-breach response)

SOC Analyst L1 Role

As a Tier-1 SOC Analyst, you are the first responder. You monitor, triage, escalate, and document every security alert.

  • Monitor SIEM, EDR/XDR, firewall, email alerts
  • Validate alerts: True Positive vs False Positive
  • Escalate critical events to Tier-2/IR
  • Log everything in ticketing systems

KPIs: Mean Time to Detect (MTTD), Response Time (MTTR), Accuracy, Escalation Quality

Training Roadmap

Phase 1: Foundation

Learn TCP/IP, DNS, ports, Windows/Linux basics, OSI model, and basic security principles like CIA triad, access control.

Phase 2: Core SOC Skills

Understand how alerts work, how to triage, investigate logs, and learn analyst workflows. Think like a defender.

Phase 3: Tools & Technologies
  • SIEM: Splunk, QRadar, Sentinel
  • EDR/XDR: CrowdStrike, Defender, Carbon Black
  • MITRE ATT&CK: Learn Tactics & Techniques
Phase 4: Threat Detection

Study common attack patterns: Phishing, Malware, Ransomware, Brute-force, Privilege Escalation, Lateral Movement.

Phase 5: Incident Response

Understand detection, escalation, containment, and reporting. Learn when and how to escalate to L2/IR teams.

Phase 6: Interview Readiness
  • Technical + scenario-based questions
  • STAR method for behavioral answers
  • Mock triage walkthroughs

Attack Use Cases

Phishing

Look for email alerts, malicious URLs, proxy logs, user reports. Confirm and quarantine.

Malware

Triggered via EDR, look for suspicious process execution. Isolate the host and escalate.

Brute Force

Many failed logins (Event 4625), usually same source IP. Block IP, reset passwords.

Interview Preparation

  • Technical: TCP/IP, DNS, SIEM, EDR, MITRE
  • Scenario: Phishing alert received – what do you do?
  • Behavioral: Describe a time you solved a hard alert

Career Path & Guidance

  • L1 → L2 → L3 → IR → Blue Team / Threat Hunter
  • Certs: Security+, CySA+, SC-200, Splunk/Core EDR certs
  • India salary: ₹3–8 LPA (entry), Global: $50K–$150K
